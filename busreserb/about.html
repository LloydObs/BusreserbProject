ABOUT US

This Pasaheroes project is an online ticket booking reservation site
which will allow you to reserve bus tickets when you wish to travel.
We offer point-to-point travel from Cubao to Baguio, Batangas, Bulacan,
Ilocos, Pampanga, La union, and Zambales and vice-versa.

For the past years, the Philippine public transport system is deemed
to be unreliable and uncomfortable by many. Thousands of passenger experience
distress and discomfort every day when commuting due to low-capacity buses in
high-demand areas. This results to the late arrival time of people to their
destinations. To ease the commutersâ€™ travel experience, as well as reducing
their time outside to minimize the risk of acquiring the virus, this project
was created.
